The Solution: Certified Partner

Executive Summary: Industrialize Power Platform delivery to transform T-Tech from a €1.7M annual deficit to €1.0M positive EBIT within 18–24 months. The solution: Certified Partner — merging Hub-and-Spoke operations with a tiered service model (Enablement/Factory/Strategic) and disctinct economic levers per tierthat fix the P&L while enabling speed and compliance.

Enablement — Retainer Model
Predictable subscription revenue per division (€250k × 4 = €1.0M) covering platform access, governance, and enablement services. Eliminates micro-invoicing chaos and aligns incentives.
Factory — Custom Components & Projects
Custom compliant components and smaller projects delivered through standardized factory execution. Business leads; T-Tech provides expertise, compliant building blocks, and delivery capacity with unit pricing and capacity blocks.
Strategic — SWAT Teams
High-value, cross-divisional initiatives with premium governance and margin capture. Target: 2–3 projects/year with €0.4–€0.6M margin per project.

Core Technical Vehicle: Governance-as-Code / Compliance-as-Code

Pre-certified building blocks (Golden Paths) make the fastest path the compliant path. Teams choose Golden Paths because they're faster (24-hour auto-approval), not because they're forced.
