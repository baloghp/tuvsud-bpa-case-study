18-month Roadmap

Rollout plan: foundations → scale → margin capture.

Takeaway: Build Platform & Governance foundation first (GaC/CaC product, visibility, governance automation), then scale the factory (offshore + Silver), then harvest margin (Gold projects).

0–3 months — Platform & Governance foundation

Platform infrastructure
  • Baseline environments, DLP policies, access model
  • Monitoring & inventory (what exists, who owns it, cost/risk)
  • Standard ALM pipeline patterns
  • Hub-and-Spoke organizational structure setup
GaC/CaC product v1
  • Pre-certified connectors/nodes (Golden Paths) for common use cases
  • Templates for approvals, audit trail, evidence automation
  • AI-powered compliance checking (RAG workflows for Golden Path validation)
  • Documentation + "how to ship safely fast" playbook
Bronze enablement
  • Maker onboarding + guardrails (controlled self-service)
  • Sandbox rules to prevent "token burning" surprises
  • Office hours + community cadence
  • Champions program setup

4–9 months — Scale factory + launch retainers

Factory capacity & labor arbitrage
  • Ramp offshore execution team (Pune/Hungary) for Silver delivery
  • Define roles: onshore architects/leads (Munich); offshore build/test
  • Establish 80/20 offshore/onshore mix for Silver tier
  • Quality gates: code review + automated compliance checks
Economic model rollout
  • Bronze: Retainer/subscription model (€250k per division × 4)
  • Silver: Capacity blocks + small project pricing
  • Define SLAs for Bronze/Silver and escalation into Gold
  • Finance reporting: cost, consumption, value delivered
Risk & value governance + backlog
  • Implement risk and value proportional governance workflow
  • Intake → Golden Path check → tiering (Bronze/Silver/Gold)
  • Launch public backlog for Silver/Gold projects with delivery estimates
  • Standard scoring: value, risk, complexity, reuse potential

10–18 months — Gold execution + margin capture

Gold pipeline & SWAT teams
  • Identify 2–3 enterprise initiatives (cross-division, high-impact)
  • Hybrid SWAT teams (70/30 offshore/onshore) with premium governance
  • Outcome metrics: ROI, risk reduction, cycle-time improvements
  • Catalog lift: harvest reusable components from Gold projects
GaC/CaC product v2 + Co-Existence
  • Expand Golden Paths to "hard" integrations and repeatable patterns
  • Make the compliant path increasingly self-service
  • Reduce manual review volume quarter over quarter
  • Implement Co-Existence Doctrine: Cap OutSystems, Grow Power Platform, Migrate Silver-class apps
P&L stabilization
  • Bronze retainers (€1M total) cover platform + steady-state ops
  • Offshore leverage (40–50% cost reduction) reduces cost base
  • Gold projects (2–3/year × €0.4M+ margin) deliver strategic impact
  • Target: €1M positive EBIT by Year 3
